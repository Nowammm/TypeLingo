TypeLingo

Primarily use "New", switching to "Old" if issues occur (as explained below).

"New" is the standard typing mode, and "Old" simulates typing using the word bank for answer checking.

"New" offers flexibility, while "Old" is stable but less flexible.

The extension isn't functioning correctly?
1. Try fully exiting Duolingo, then relaunch.
2. If needed, temporarily switch to "Old" until a bug fix is available.
